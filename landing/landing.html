Ego
A new way to surf the Internet
You use Internet to gain information about your interests. ...and you visit many, many websites to gain such information.

But why visit websites? Are they your ultimate purpose of using the Internet?
Websites are just means to get information about your interests. Now is the time to get right into your real goal: Interests.

Ego makes it easier for you to receive information about your interests. There is no need to go through hundreds of websites everyday.
Just discover, subscribe, and keep your interest - all in one place - on Ego.

Welcome to your new Internet.
Feed
Subscribe to your interests
What are you interested in? Technology? K-Pop? Ferrari? Whatever it is, just subscribe to your interests with a simple click. From general subjects like "Technology" to something as specific as "iPhone jailbreak," you will be updated with the latest information about your interest every minute.

Information you receive on Internet is not just news. You can find information of all sorts - from data and shopping mall entries to user forum posts and chats on social network. Interested in iPhone? Subscribing will open your mind to latest news, offers, and even nearby vendors and current used prices, or what other people are talking about.
Front Page
Discover your interests
Sometimes you don't know what your interest is, or may be you want to find new interests. Front Page is here for you.
See what are the most popular articles on the Internet - of all kinds of topics: Technology, Music, Soccer, and so on.

What topics are your friends interested in right now? Nearby? On Front Page, you can see trending keywords from around the world, nation, friends, or nearby.

Discover what other sources you might be interested in - interested in Technology? How about Engadget? Front Page will recommend new websites and sources that you might add as your "friend."
Clips
Keep your interests
So you found an article that you love. How about adding it to your Clip? Whether it is a news entry about the latest iPhone rumor, chic Ferrari cologne on Amazon, or your girlfriend's Facebook status, you can add them all to your own collage of interests.

But, let's not be selfish. Let's share some love - of information. You can share articles to your friends' clip, or their social network accounts like Facebook, Twitter, and Google+.

We all love stalking. You can look at your friends' clips - see what they like, and maybe add an article that to your clip as well. At the same time, we hate being "stalked." You can easily mark your articles as Private with a simple click of a button. It's a win-win for everyone.
